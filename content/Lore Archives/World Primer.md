The world you're entering into was in the midst of a magical revolution. Spearheaded by those at [[The Grand Luminarium]] in [[Calomir]], but when [[Nightfall]] struck, a world at one point in balance became unstable. The sun vanished, the stars froze, and the boundaries between realities frayed. In the days(?) since, Light has become the measure of life itself. The darker the road ahead grows, the closer the world bends toward ruin.

---
# The State of the World
The collapse was swift. Entire nations vanished when the light went out.  

The geography of the world is still recognizable, but its rules are not. The dark reshapes what it touches: forests knot into spirals, rivers harden into mirrored glass, and cities crumble beneath skies that no longer age.  

Only [[Calomir]] endures, its false sun burning above the city like a defiant star. Beyond its glow lies nothing mapped, nothing safe, and nothing certain.

---
# The Nature of Light
Light has become the symbol of life itself. The [[Nyxwraiths]], manifest shadows born of [[Nightfall]], cannot bear it.  

Every expedition carries lightstones, lanterns, or spells to hold the dark at bay, yet every flame is borrowed time. When the last light fades, the world bends inward, swallowing sound and color until only gray remains.

The scholars of [[The Grand Luminarium]] has imparted the [[Duskrunner Guild]] with artifice to hold the shadows at bay. But where one danger is averted, another may show its face...
